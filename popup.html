Welcome!

I'm glad you've decided to adopt your own personal Mudge! Always zipping around, the Mudge likes to stay focused. And it likes to help you stay focused too! To add distracting sites for the Mudge to keep an eye out for, press on the site book and type them in along with the ideal amount of time you'd like to spend on them. Don't forget to press submit! The Mudge will count down every time you open/refresh the site, patiently waiting before splashing your screen if you exceed your time limit! And don't try to scroll past; it'll just muddy it up more! Take the time to press each splotch to clean up the screen and reclaim your focus while at it. Since this is a beta version, time can only be inputted in as seconds. Sorry! Happy focusing!
